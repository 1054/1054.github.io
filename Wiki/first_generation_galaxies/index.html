Timeline of the Universe

Timeline of the Universe

According to The Big Bang Theory + Inflation Theory:

Name of Epoch Time of Epoch Redshift Description
Planck Epoch 0 to $10^{-43}$ s   4in1 fundamental force
Inflationary Epoch unknown to $10^{-32}$ s   inflaton accelerates cosmic expansion
Quark Epoch $10^{-12}$ to $10^{-6}$ s   elementary particles interact Higgs field then got mass
Hadron Epoch $10^{-6}$ to 1 s   hardons including baryons form from quark-gluon plasma
Lepton Epoch 1 to 10 s   most hadrons annihilate and leptons dominate in mass
Photon Epoch 10 s to 380,000 years   most leptons annihilate and photons dominate in energy
Nucleosynthesis 3 min to 20 min   atom ions form from protons neutrons => H:He=3:1 in mass
Matter Domination 70,000 years to now   cold dark matter dominates amplifing the inhomogeneities
Recombination 377,000 years to now z~1050 to 0 after quick proton-electron recombination, photons become free to travel
Reionization 150 Myr to 1 Gyr z~22 to 5~6 first stars and quasars form, reionizing surroundings, until all H with certain low density (e.g. IGM) are ionized
First Star ~ 180 Myr z~22 The first star, i.e. PopIII with extremely low metallicity, was found by ESA as reported by BBC News in Feb 2015. New evdidence of the first star was found by Bowman, Rogers, Monsalve, Mozdzen & Mahesh (2018)
First Black Hole ~ 500 Myr z~10 The first black hole was found by Richard Ellis (?)
First Galaxy ~ 500 Myr z~10 Planck CMB lead to the evidence that galaxies shine at z~10 (500 Myr).
